Our Mission

Founded on the principle that quality journalism is essential to a functioning democracy, The Adriatic provides clear, accurate, and thoughtful analysis of the events shaping our interconnected world.

We believe that informed citizens make better decisions, and our role is to ensure that the public has access to the information they need to understand complex global challenges.
